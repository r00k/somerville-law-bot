The only provided ordinance on this topic addresses **private parking lot sweeping**, not the citywide street-sweeping or alternate-side parking schedule most residents are asking about.

Under Sec. 12-20.1, parking lots may not be swept between 7:00 p.m. and 7:00 a.m. on weekdays, and not between 7:00 p.m. and 9:00 a.m. on Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays (Sec. 12-20.1(a)). This is essentially a noise/nuisance-timing rule limiting when sweeping machines can operate in lots.

Violations are handled as a noncriminal disposition under state law (G.L. c. 40, § 21D), with each day of violation counting as a separate offense subject to the fine set in subsection 1-11(b) (Sec. 12-20.1(b)). The city may also pursue court-ordered relief and seek costs and attorney's fees.

**Gotchas / gaps:** The provided text does **not** cover residential street-sweeping schedules, alternate-side parking rules, posted "no parking for street cleaning" signs, ticketing, or towing during street sweeping.
